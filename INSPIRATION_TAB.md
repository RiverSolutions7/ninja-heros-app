# NINJA H.E.R.O.S. COACH HUB — INSPIRATION TAB

---

## THE PROBLEM THIS SOLVES

Coaches are constantly discovering great ideas.

A drill spotted on an Instagram Reel. A conditioning game
from a YouTube Short. A warmup sequence shared in a TikTok.
A coach sees it, thinks "that's perfect for my Junior Ninjas"
— and then texts it to themselves, saves it to a camera roll,
or just tries to remember it.

A week later it's gone. Buried in a chat thread. Lost in a
scroll. Never made it into a class.

This happens to every coach, at every gym, constantly. The
ideas are out there. The problem is that there is no system
for capturing them.

**The Inspiration tab exists to change that.**

---

## WHAT IT IS

A dedicated inbox for coaching ideas found anywhere on the
internet.

A coach pastes a link — YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, or
anything else. The app pulls the thumbnail and title
automatically. The coach adds a few tags and a short note.
Done in under a minute.

Everything lives in one organized, searchable place inside
the same app they already use to log classes.

---

## WHAT IT IS NOT

This is not a video downloader. The app never stores the
video itself — only the link, the metadata, and the coach's
own notes. The content stays where it lives on the internet.

This is also not a social feed or a content discovery tool.
Coaches bring the ideas in. The app organizes them.

---

## WHY IT BELONGS IN THIS APP

The Coach Hub is a program intelligence system. Its job is
to capture everything that makes a program work — classes,
skills, games, setups, and now the raw inspiration that
feeds all of those things.

Without the Inspiration tab, great ideas enter a coach's
brain and leave through a text message. With it, they enter
the system and stay there — tagged, searchable, and
available to every coach who uses the app.

This is the same principle as the class library. The more
that gets captured, the more valuable the system becomes.

---

## THE DESIRED OUTCOME

A coach should be able to:

1. See something worth saving anywhere on the internet
2. Paste the link into the app in under 60 seconds
3. Tag it with one or two words (warmup, balance, Elite Ninjas)
4. Find it again in 10 seconds when planning a class
5. Eventually pull it into a logged class and mark it as used

When that loop works consistently, the Inspiration tab becomes
the bridge between what coaches discover and what actually
happens on the gym floor.

---

## HOW TO MEASURE IF IT'S WORKING

Three questions. That's it.

- Did a coach save something they would have otherwise
  texted to themselves?
- Did they find it again when they needed it?
- Did it make it into a class?

If those three things happen — even once — the feature works.

---

## WHAT COMES NEXT (NOT NOW)

These are ideas for later versions — not the first build.

- **Mark as tried** — link an inspiration clip to a logged
  class once it's been used on the gym floor
- **Share with coaches** — send a clip to another coach in
  the same gym directly from the app

None of these belong in the first version. The first version
just needs to capture ideas reliably and make them findable.
Get that right first.

---

## DESIGN PRINCIPLES FOR THIS FEATURE

- **One minute or it won't get used** — the save flow must
  be faster than texting it to yourself
- **Tags over folders** — free-form tags are faster than
  navigating a category system
- **Links, not downloads** — never store video content,
  only references to it
- **Graceful when links break** — content gets deleted,
  accounts go private. The app handles this cleanly without
  breaking the card
- **Same app, same feel** — this tab should feel native to
  the Coach Hub, not like a feature bolted on after the fact

---

*Measure every future decision about this feature against
one question: does this make it faster and easier for a
coach to capture a good idea and find it again later?
If not, don't build it.*
